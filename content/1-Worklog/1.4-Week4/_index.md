### Week 4 Objectives:

* Understand identity and security concepts in AWS.
* Learn how to create and manage IAM users, groups, and roles.
* Understand policies and permission management.
* Explore Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
* Build a secure multi-user AWS environment.
