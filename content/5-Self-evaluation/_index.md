During my 12-week internship as a **Cloud Intern (AWS)**, I had the opportunity to explore and practice with a wide range of AWS services, from foundational topics to more advanced areas such as serverless architecture, containers, DevOps, security, monitoring, and system optimization. This journey helped me expand not only my technical knowledge but also my ability to think about cloud systems in a more structured and practical way.

Throughout the internship, I worked with services such as EC2, S3, RDS, VPC, IAM, Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, SQS, SNS, ECS, CloudWatch, CloudFormation, and CodePipeline. Through these activities, I strengthened my ability to learn new technologies, implement cloud-based solutions, understand how AWS services work together, and gradually develop a system-oriented mindset rather than studying each service in isolation.

In addition to technical growth, I also improved my self-learning ability, teamwork, openness to feedback, and capacity to connect technical knowledge with practical use cases. At the same time, I recognize that I still need to improve in several areas, especially technical depth, discipline, real-world problem solving, and professional communication.

### Key Strengths

- Built a solid breadth of knowledge across important AWS service domains  
- Learned quickly and connected multiple services into a broader system view  
- Showed initiative in practicing and exploring beyond theoretical concepts  
- Began developing a system design, DevOps, and cloud operations mindset  
- Maintained a positive learning attitude and willingness to improve through feedback  

### Areas for Improvement

- Need to go deeper into selected areas instead of remaining mostly broad in coverage  
- Need to improve discipline and maintain a more consistent working rhythm  
- Need to strengthen real-world problem-solving skills, especially in performance, cost, and operational issues  
- Need to improve professional communication, particularly in presenting solutions and explaining technical choices  

### Development Direction After the Internship

Moving forward, I would like to continue growing toward a **Cloud/DevOps Engineer** path by improving technical depth, building more realistic hands-on projects, and strengthening my ability to design production-ready systems. I also aim to become better at analyzing trade-offs, optimizing systems, and communicating technical ideas more effectively in professional environments.
