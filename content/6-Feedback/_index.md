>This section is intended to capture personal reflections and feedback from participating in the First Cloud Journey program, as well as suggestions to help the FCJ team improve and enhance future experiences.

### General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is comfortable and welcoming. Team members are supportive and willing to help when needed, even outside of working hours. The workspace is organized, which helps maintain focus. However, organizing more team bonding or informal activities could further strengthen connections among members.

**2. Support from Mentors / Admin Team**  
Mentors provide clear guidance and are always open to questions. They encourage independent problem-solving before offering direct solutions, which helps improve learning. The admin team also supports well in terms of documentation and processes, making the workflow smoother.

**3. Relevance to Academic Background**  
The tasks assigned are generally aligned with what I have learned at university, while also introducing new areas. This balance helps reinforce foundational knowledge and expand practical experience.

**4. Learning Opportunities & Skill Development**  
During the internship, I had the chance to develop various skills, including teamwork, task management, and professional communication. Insights shared by mentors also helped me better understand real-world applications and shape my career direction.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**  
The team culture is positive and collaborative. Members respect each other and work effectively together, especially during high-pressure situations. This made it easier for me to integrate into the team despite being an intern.

**6. Internship Policies & Benefits**  
The program provides internship allowances and offers flexibility when needed. Additionally, access to internal training sessions is a valuable benefit that supports continuous learning.
